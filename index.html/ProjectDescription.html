Project Description

1. Overview
• Topic

In a restless world, most people seem to ignore their health by fasting and just concentrate on their study, work or taking care of their family because they did not have time to eat properly. Whereas some are more likely to become lazy and all they do is eat and rest the whole day without any outdoor activities. From that, many types of diseases were born such as obesity, malnutrition, and appendicitis. Therefore, we would like to create an application which helps users to have a healthy lifestyle with balanced diet and beneficial body exercises by providing services like body measurement to select appropriate diets and activities suitable for the users.

If this project becomes successful, we hope to reduce the number of people with poor physical health worldwide. Moreover, this application is also expected to have significant advantages for the medical industry as it collects and stores data which will be delivered to the World Health Organization.

• Motivation

Physical health is human’s most valuable asset because without it, there is no energy and strength for humans to work at all. Apparently, people would be more likely to be careless about their health and eventually they will have some serious illnesses. This is also our group project's main motivation as we believe that our project will be able to help a lot of people who have poor physical health. Moreover, we want to contribute to the development of the health sector by providing advanced technologies.

• Landscape

Making a health application specifically physical health is a common idea among potential developers around the world. Therefore, ideas being duplicated and creating similar applications are inevitable situations. There are a number of apps that are identical to our project idea such as: Noom, MyPlate Calories Counter,etc. In order to solve this problem, our application is required to have uniqueness in functions or design. But because of the sheer number of apps, we can not tell if those apps already have that functionality or not. As a result, we would use some features like a live chat box with professional doctors or an AI assistant that remind users to do their tasks in exercises and eating routine. In detail, live chat box will provide live chatting with doctors during office hours for users to seek advice or questions that they want to ask directly at home without going to the clinic. And an AI assistant will not only remind users when the time has come to do the task but it will also include some encouraging words to motivate users. However, since we are still newbies in the IT field, it is not easy to add those features into the app. So we would do more research on how to implement those functions and hopefully we could accomplish it in the future.

2. Detailed Description
• Aims
Obesity has become a global epidemic for decades and it causes many negative effects toward the human body. According to WHO: “
- “Worldwide obesity has nearly tripled since 1975.
- In 2016, more than 1.9 billion adults, 18 years and older, were overweight. Of these over 650 million were obese.
- 39% of adults aged 18 years and over were overweight in 2016, and 13% were obese.
- Most of the world's population live in countries where overweight and obesity kills more people than underweight.
- 38 million children under the age of 5 were overweight or obese in 2019.
- Over 340 million children and adolescents aged 5-19 were overweight or obese in 2016.
- Obesity is preventable. ” [1]

Although obesity is a health problem which most people get around the world, it is not difficult to search for solutions to prevent as well as curing this illness. Therefore, our group intention is to create an application that could help users to select their proper diet and exercises for their body in order to lose weight. In specific, we have two main goals for this project. One would be providing a diverse menu of diet and exercise plans so that users can have a wide range of choices which suit their body condition best. Another goal is to reduce the amount of overweight people globally and help the health care industry to develop.

Suppose this project could not be able to complete due to the time limitation, the most important outcome that we expected to output is for the user to lose weight successfully with our plans. Nothing is more important than improving health and this is also our number one priority when implementing the project. Everyone can lose weight without exercising because with a variety of diet menus, people can choose which diet is effective for them as humans need to eat so as to live. Not everyone can spend time to go to the gym and get fit or even exercise at home since most people are too busy with their work, studies or taking care of their family. Therefore, we are more likely to concentrate on the diet menu rather than the exercise plans.

References:
[1]: https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/obesity-and-overweight

• Plans and Progress

At the beginning of the project, we started by dividing the project into two parts: report writing and creating the application. We assigned each member to work on different parts of the project. For the application, two people will create contents, specifically searching for numerous diet plans and exercises to put into the application. The other two members will work on forming the app, in other words they will make and design the basic outline for it. About the report, since it contains many parts that need to be written, we divide each person an equal amount of sections that one person has to write. Next up, we created a Github group repository so that everyone can have access and contribute their work in it. We also set up a google document for members to insert their writing for the report. For most of the time, we communicate with one another via Facebook Messenger in our group chat, where we plan, inform what each of us have done throughout the week and exchange information about the project. We used to make an appointment so that the whole team could meet up and discuss further about how this project would be completed. However, some problems occurred such as there was a member who said that he was sick and could not participate in the meeting. Another member said that he also could not attend the meeting due to his personal family issues. Therefore, we decided to meet each other in the tutorial class because it is much easier to do so.

Everything was proceeding very well as everyone of us participated and made small effort for the assignment day by day. Suddenly, one of our members determined to leave the group by reason of incompatible working styles. Because that member left the group abruptly, we fell into a state of confusion where we do not know what to do next. The member who left was considered as the core of the group as she led us and told us what to do. Moreover, the project idea also belongs to her so that she is the person who can visualize the expected outcome of the work. With the deadline for submissions coming close, we had no choice but to continue with this project since we do not have enough time to think and create another practical project like this application. We would change a few aspects of the idea so that it will be different from the original one to avoid plagiarism. And since the number of members have been cut down from four to three, we have to speed up the progress of the work as we need to cover the leaver remaining parts. Not only were we exhausted from increasing the amount of work, but our mental health was affected with stress and pressure. However, we are still trying hard to complete the assignment and we also comfort as well as motivate each other to work harder.

Since there is not much time left and having membership shortage with loads of work need to be done, three main jobs will be divided as one will write the remaining parts of the report, one will create the group website and set up Github repository and the last one will work on making the basic features of the app. We hope that we can finish all of the work before the deadline and for the presentation, we will make Powerpoint slides, record our voices and then we will use a screen-recording software and a video editor to insert our voices into the video.
