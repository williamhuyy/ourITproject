Team Profile

1. Team Name

Team name is one of the most essential factors for a group work as it helps distinguish different teams and through the name, we can express our personalities along with showing our uniqueness. After a long discussion, having many names for us to consider, we decided to choose the name “WAF”. WAF stands for “we are family” and we wish to express our connection between each other as a family. And although this name sounds very homely and simple, it shows that the atmosphere in the group is very warm and peaceful.
